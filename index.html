Osteopathy in your Own Home

Osteopathy is a highly effective form of physical therapy. We identify and treat the root cause of musculoskeletal pain, not just the symptoms, restoring normal function and movement to allow the body to repair and heal itself.

On The Move Osteopathy frees you from the need to factor in travel times to and from your appointment as the treatment is provided in your own home. This makes our mobile service ideal for those who work from home, those with young children, those who are in too much pain to easily travel or indeed anyone who prefers to maximise their time in a day by having us come to you.

We are based in Enfield, and cover the surrounding area from Crews Hill to Wood Green and from Oakleigh Park to Ponders End
