FAQs

How do I make an appointment?

Please visit our Contact Us page for contact details and phone, email or send a message via our contact form. We also welcome general enquiries by phone, email and contact form.

Do I need a GPs referral?

No. Osteopaths are knows as Primary Healthcare professionals and are full trained to identify symptoms that are nor musculoskeletal and will tell you if you need to be seen by your GP. Some health insurance companies may however require a GP referral before claiming on the policy, please check with your provider.

Can I pay with credit/debit card?

Wireless card payment is planned for the near future, but currently I can only accept cash for payment.

How many treatments will I need?

This depends on the diagnosis of the condition, how long you have had it, general health and how you respond to treatment. It is likely that you will require a short course of treatment for all but the most simple of issues. I'll never prescribe a set number of treatments or "block book" sessions, but will do my best to estimate recovert time within the first one or two appointments.

Do I need to prepare anything before you arrive?

Yes please, as a mobile service I have a few requirements listed in What I'll Require. Thank You.
