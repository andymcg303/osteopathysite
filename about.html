ANDY McGOWAN BOst

Andy is an experienced and professional osteopath registered with the General Osteopathic Council. He graduated from the UKs largest osteopathic educational establishment, the British School of Osteopathy in 2007 and has over a decade of experience in osteopathic general practice.

Andy is dedicated to diagnosing and treating the root cause of musculoskeletal pain and dysfunction, and effectively treats a wide range of muscle and joint problems using the key osteopathic techniques of massage, mobilisation and manipulation for the back, neck and other areas of the body. He will always provide you with a programme of rehabilitation exercises so you can manage and self-treat your injury at home between treatment sessions, speeding healing and aiding recovery.

He also has additional training in Medical Acupuncture for pain relief and muscle relaxation; and in the optimisation of functional movement and “posture” to prevent adverse muscle tension recurring.

Andy is registered with all major private health insurance providers including Bupa, AXA, Aviva, Cigna, Vitality, Simplyhealth and WPA.
